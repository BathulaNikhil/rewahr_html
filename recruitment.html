Recruitment Management

Recruitment Management is a module that helps organizations to streamline the process of hiring new employees. It helps to manage the entire recruitment process from creating job requisitions to hiring employees. It helps to automate the recruitment process and reduce the time and effort required to hire new employees.

Using Rewa HR’s Recruitment Management module, you can create job requisitions, post job openings on your website, and track applicants through the entire recruitment process. You can also create and manage a database of applicants, and track the status of each applicant throughout the recruitment process. The module also allows you to create and manage a database of job descriptions, and track the status of each job opening.
